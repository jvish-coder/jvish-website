Who We Are

Founder-Led.
Full Ownership.
Zero Handoffs.

Most event companies coordinate. They make calls, book vendors, manage timelines. Jvish does all that — and then our team physically builds what you brief. Booth structures, signage, stage sets, branded installations. Our workshop, our tools, our hands.

And we don't specialise in one event type. You name it — roadshow, carnival, launch, exhibition, walkathon, dinner — we've built it, run it, and packed it up. Tell us what you need. We'll make it happen.

We don't wait to be told what to do. We listen, think, and propose. That's how a forklift distributor gets a full brand launch experience they never knew how to ask for.
