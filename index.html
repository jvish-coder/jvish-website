What We Do

Seven Specialisations.
One Accountable Team.

End-to-end event management and production supported by our in-house production team — from concept to completion.

🎯

Brand Activations & Roadshows

On-ground consumer engagement in malls and high-footfall locations. Booth design, fabrication, staffing, sampling, and sales activities — all managed end-to-end.

🎤

PR Launches & Product Launches

Full-service launch events for brands entering markets. Venue, stage, AV, talent handling, media setup, guest experience, and custom stage sets.

Exhibition & Trade Show Booths

Professional exhibition booth setup from concept to completion. Custom carpentry, branded graphics, signage, lighting — built, installed, and dismantled in-house.

Corporate Events
