What We Do

Eight Specialisations.
One Accountable Team.

End-to-end event management and production across every format — from mall activations to gala dinners — supported by our in-house production team.

Brand Activations & Roadshows

On-ground consumer engagement in shopping malls and high-footfall locations. Booth design, fabrication, staffing, consumer mechanics, sampling, and sales activities — all managed end-to-end.

PR Launches & Product Launches

Full-service launch events for brands introducing products or entering new markets. Venue, stage, AV, celebrity or talent handling, media setup, guest experience, and custom stage sets.

Exhibition & Trade Show Booths

Professional exhibition booth setup from concept to completion. Custom carpentry, branded graphics, signage, lighting, AV — built, installed, and dismantled by our in-house team.

Corporate Events

Appreciation dinners, award nights, annual dinners, and internal gatherings. Full management of venue, staging, custom set design, AV, lighting, guest experience, and registration.

Carnivals & Walkathons

Bespoke themed carnivals, community walkathons, leisure events, and outdoor activations. Themed structures, props, power supply, and full event logistics.

Conferences & Community Gatherings

End-to-end conference production including staging, AV, registration systems, printed materials, and flow management for large seated audiences.

Event Coordination & Project Management

For clients who need a capable coordinator to manage vendors, timelines, logistics, and on-ground execution for competitions, multi-party events, or complex productions.

Sound, Lighting & Set Design

Comprehensive AV production and creative set design for any event scale — from intimate corporate functions to large gala dinners and outdoor stages. Custom-built sets designed and fabricated in-house.
